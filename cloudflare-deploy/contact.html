Frequently Asked Questions

📦 What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Most products have a minimum order quantity of 50-100 pieces. For custom orders, we support small batch trial orders. For specific MOQ, please consult our sales team.

⏱️ What is the production cycle?

In-stock products typically ship within 3-7 days. Customized products need 15-30 days, specifically depends on order quantity and complexity.

🎨 Do you support OEM/ODM customization?

Yes! We have a professional design team, supporting customization according to your needs. Including product design, packaging design, and more.

✅ What quality guarantees do you offer?

We accept ISO 9001 quality certification, all products undergo strict quality inspection. Support quality assurance, if you have any questions, please contact us.

💳 What payment methods do you support?

We support T/T, PayPal, Western Union and other payment methods. For new customers, usually requires 30% deposit, 70% pay before shipping.

🚚 Do you support international delivery?

Yes! We have extensive international logistics experience, supporting global delivery. Can help handle customs clearance, shipping and other logistics.
