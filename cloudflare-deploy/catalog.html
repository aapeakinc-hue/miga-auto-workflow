Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our catalog and products

Is the catalog free to download?
Yes! Our catalog is completely free. Simply enter your email and you'll receive instant access to the PDF file.
How often is the catalog updated?
Our catalog is updated annually. The 2025 version features all new products, updated pricing, and the latest design trends.
Are the prices in the catalog accurate?
Yes, all prices in the catalog are current factory direct prices. However, final pricing may vary based on customization requirements, order quantity, and current material costs.
Can I order samples from the catalog?
Absolutely! We offer free samples (up to 3 products). You only pay for shipping. Contact us to request samples of any products from the catalog.
Do you offer customization?
Yes, we offer full OEM/ODM services. We can customize design, size, color, and even add your logo. Our design team provides free consultation for custom orders.
What is your minimum order quantity?
MOQ varies by product. Most items have an MOQ of 50-100 pieces. Custom orders typically have an MOQ of 100 pieces. Please check the catalog for specific MOQ information.
How long is the lead time?
For in-stock items: 3-5 business days. For custom orders: 15-20 business days. Expedited shipping is available for urgent orders.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. We work with reliable international shipping partners including DHL, FedEx, and UPS to ensure safe and timely delivery.
